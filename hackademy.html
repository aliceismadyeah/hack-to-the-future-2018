Hackademy

PRESENT TO WIN!


You don't need to know coding to build a winning demo presentation. Get ready for hands on workshops to learn how to detail out your Hackathon idea, build interactive mockups that look great and present your idea clearly. Sign up for classes here: bit.ly/1lO18DY

Here are the classes we offer:

I Have and Idea, Now What?

July 8th, 6-7pm

By the end of this class, you’ll know how to create a storyboard that details your idea. In this workshop you will learn how to clearly define your problem, check out the competition, and sketch your solution. The final product will be pitch deck including a storyboard of your idea.

Mock it Up!

July 9th, 6-7pm

By the end of this class you’ll know how to create a prototype using Keynote, Powerpoint or Google presentations. In this workshop you will practice placing screenshots in the program, changing parts of the screenshots, using transitions, and findings resources. The final product will be a clickable prototype.

Give a Kicka$$ Presentation

July 10th, 6-7pm

By the end of this class you’ll learn the elements of giving a successfull 2-minute presentation. In this workshop you will practice in small teams and create an outline. The final result will be CONFIDENCE TO WIN!
