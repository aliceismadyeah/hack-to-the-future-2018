Judging Criteria

Only teams with people from at least 3 different departments will be eligible to present and to win prizes. Solo hackers and teams with members from fewer departments are still welcome to hack.

Departments, this year, are defined as:

  • Tech (Engineers / Tech Ops / QA / BI)
  • Marketing (Regional Directors / Marketing Design / Blog / PR / Email / Events)
  • Product (Product Managers / UX Designers / Researchers / GMs)
  • Content (Reviewers / Researchers / Editors / Contributor Support)
  • Sales (AE / AM / Sales)
  • Operations (Finance / Legal / HR / Biz Dev)

    • Register your team today at the registration page. The deadline for team registration is Friday July 18th at 9:00am EST.

    Judging Categories

    This year, all hacks must be submitted under one of the following categories (hacks are only eligible to win one of these three):

  • Customer Impact
  • Contributor Impact
  • Employee Value

    • Additionally, all hacks will be eligible to win one of these overall categories:

  • Best Overall
  • Most Humorous
  • Best Non Code-based hack
  • People's Choice

    • After presentations finish at the demo event, judges will meet and select winners. During this time, we'll also have a survey for the company to vote for the People's Choice award. All winning hacks will be announced at the end of the demo event.
