Judging Criteria

Only teams who fit the following criteria will be eligible to present and win prizes.

Pitch Team Criteria:

Employees can submit a pitch before the main Hack to the Future event. Pitch submissions will be shortlisted by a panel of judges to demo their idea at the Hack kick off event, encouraging Hack teams to work on your idea during Hack to the Future.

  • At least 3 people on the team.
  • Can not enter/win in more than one category.
  • Exactly 1 minute to pitch.
  • Submit a completed 1 minute pitch deck and/or video by Wednesday, November 2 11:00am ET.
  • Pitch ideas that are then hacked on will receive a prize

Hack Team Criteria:

  • At least 4 people from 4 different departments on the team.
  • Must build and present a coded prototype.
  • Can not enter/win in more than one category.
  • Exactly 2 minutes to demo.
  • Submit your team and demo details by Friday, November 4 at 8:00am ET

You can recruit as many people as you like for your team!

Departments are defined as:

  • Tech (Developers, Engineers, Tech Ops, QA, BI, IT)
  • Marketing (Creative, Content Marketing, SEO, Product Marketing, Media, CRM, Comms, Events, Global Customer Care, etc.)
  • Product (Product Managers, UX Designers, Researchers)
  • Content (Acquisition, Researchers, Editors)
  • Sales, Biz Dev (AE, AM, SAE)
  • Operations (Finance, Legal, People, PPMO)

Register your team today on the Pitch registration page or Hack registration page. The deadline for Pitch team registration is Wednesday, November 2 at 11:00am ET, and the deadline for Hack team registration is Friday, November 4 at 8:00am ET.

Judges

  • Jeff Weiser, Chief Marketing Officer
  • Sandeep Chouksey, VP, Engineering
  • Sarah Haq, Director, Analytics
  • Janet Giesen, VP, Business Development
  • Paul Brennan, VP, Content Operations and Contributor Success

Judging Categories

All hacks and pitches should be offer solutions for customer impact. A customer is a buyer or a seller. Hacks should focus on one or more areas e.g.

  • Solve the top problems for our Enterprise customers
  • Enable our global customers find content faster than any other platform.

Each submission is only eligible to win in one category. The categories will be revealed by the judges soon!

After Hack demos finish on Friday, November 4, judges will deliberate and then announce the winners via live stream the same day.

Prizes:

The winning teams will receive a prize (details TBD) and their hack/pitch will be considered for a sprint in 2017.
