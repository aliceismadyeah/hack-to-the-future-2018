FAQ

Logistics

Does this run all night?
Hack to the Future runs 24 hours from noon to noon. The office is open all night, and you can sleep if you want! Project demo presentations will then take place during the afternoon of Friday, July 26.

Do we have to work through the entire 24 hour period?
No, you can work on your project as much as you want.


Will you provide snacks and refreshments?
Yes!


Is it mandatory? No, but we strongly encourage everyone to participate.


What if my regular job duties prevent me from participating? Please talk to your manager to see if together you can make it work. You may participate for just part of the day if that works better.

Does my project have to "work?" No, you can have your project as realized as you want it to be, from an initial comp to a fully working prototype.

Can I work from home/out of office? Yes, but only with the approval of your manager.
